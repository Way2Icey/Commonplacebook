Courage to Act

Jephthah and Gideon (referenced in St. George) "They led their men to fight... and put them all to flight."

Jephtah and gideon are definitive personas of courageous leaders who led their men to fight. Within this quote you can reveal the true message of being triumphant within valor and faith. The tale of saint george paints a picture of decisive victory and triumph over great challenge. The quote defines courage to act in the terms of great leaders understanding crucial position and acting surgically in them. Ultimately, it shows a deep message about having the courage to act being rooted in purpose and has the power to change while leaving a long lasting legacy of hope and triumph.
